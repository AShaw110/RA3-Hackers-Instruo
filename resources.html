Brain Games

Engaging in brain games can be a fun way to reduce stress and sharpen your cognitive skills. Here are some games designed to help you relax while keeping your mind active:

  • MindLight - Stress & Anxiety Management Game

    MindLight is a scientifically proven game that helps kids manage their stress and anxiety. It uses a biofeedback system to help players learn relaxation techniques while having fun.

  • Cognifit - Improve Cognitive Health

    Cognifit offers various cognitive games that aim to boost memory, attention, and problem-solving skills. These activities can be relaxing while providing brain exercises.

  • Max and the Magic Marker - CVG for Anxiety

    This creative video game encourages players to use their imagination to solve problems, offering a fun, calming experience that can help with general anxiety.
